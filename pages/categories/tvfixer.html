Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical TV repair take? +

Most common TV repairs can be completed within 1-2 hours on-site. Complex issues requiring part replacements might take 24-48 hours depending on part availability.

Do you offer warranty on repairs? +

Yes, all our repairs come with a 90-day warranty covering both parts and labor. If the same issue reoccurs within this period, we'll fix it at no additional cost.

What brands do you repair? +

We repair all major TV brands including Samsung, LG, Sony, TCL, Vizio, Hisense, Panasonic, Toshiba, and more. Our technicians are trained on a wide variety of models.

Is it worth repairing an old TV? +

We provide a free diagnosis and will advise you if repair is cost-effective. Generally, if repair costs are less than 50% of a comparable new TV, it's worth fixing.
